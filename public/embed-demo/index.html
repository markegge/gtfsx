How this works

The maps and schedule tables on this page aren't manually maintained — they're embedded directly from our GTFS feed using a single <iframe> tag per widget. When we update our schedule and re-publish the feed, the embeds update automatically. No more out-of-date PDFs. No more "the website says one thing, the bus does another."

This is a demo of the GTFS·X embed feature. Every transit agency that maintains their feed in GTFS·X gets these widgets for free, ready to paste into WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, or hand-rolled HTML like this page.
