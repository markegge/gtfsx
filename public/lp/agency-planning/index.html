FAQ

How does GTFS·X Agency compare to Remix?
Same core map-based planning, demographics, and Title VI. ~1/6 the annual cost ($2,499 vs. $20K+). Trade-off: GTFS·X is leaner — fewer enterprise features (multi-user permissioning, white-label, professional services) — but the planning fundamentals are equivalent. See /compare/remix/ for details.
Can I try the Agency features without signing up?
Yes. The editor is free; demographics and Title VI analysis run inside a free trial of the Agency tier. No credit card required.
What's included in Agency tier?
Route planning + demographics + Title VI + cost estimation + Pro hosting features. Multi-org membership (good for consultants serving multiple agencies). Email support.
Do you offer professional services?
No. We're software. If you need consulting, we can refer you to AICP-credentialed planners (including the founder).
What about FTA reporting requirements?
Title VI equity analysis is built in. Disparate-impact and disparate-burden reports follow FTA Circular 4702.1B structure. NTD-ready service-hour reporting is on the roadmap.
What if we outgrow the Agency tier?
The product scales to large-agency volumes. The "outgrow" path is usually adding enterprise features (SSO, audit logs, dedicated support), which we'd add as needed. Talk to us before you decide you've outgrown it.
